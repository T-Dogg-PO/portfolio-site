About Thomas Tremlett

I am a web developer originally from New Zealand, but currently living in Luxembourg.

Although I started off my career with a Masters Degree in Materials Engineering, I had the opportunity to stumble into the IT industry while working in Vancouver, Canada. Here I fell in love with web development and choose to make the career switch. Through a combination of my own self-taught learning and the CareerFoundry Full Stack Web Development course, I'm now looking for my first developer positon!

With almost two years of experience working in Customer Support, I understand the importance of customer obsessed development. It's my belief that my role as a developer is to help enable a users success, and I attempt to bring that philosophy to my coding every day.

I am most familiar with the MERN stack (MongoDB, Express, React, Node), but I also have experience with Angular, TypeScript and Python (particularly the Django framework).

When I'm not coding or travelling, I spend my free time swimming (long distance open water) or gaming (Age of Empires 2 and Baldur's Gate).
